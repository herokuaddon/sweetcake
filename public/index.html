Beginner Baking Guide

Classic Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F or 175°C.
  2. Grease and line two cake pans.
  3. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, then mix until smooth.
  5. Carefully stir in hot water.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool completely before frosting.

Baking Advice

Helpful Cake Baking Tips

Measure Ingredients Carefully

Accurate measurements help cakes rise properly and keep the texture soft, moist, and balanced.

Do Not Overmix Batter

Mix only until ingredients are combined. Overmixing can make cakes dense or tough.

Cool Before Frosting

Let cakes cool fully before adding frosting, or the frosting may melt and slide off.
